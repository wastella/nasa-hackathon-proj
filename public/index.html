LLM Use Case

The Large Language Model part of our application uses Large Language Models to perform advanced analysis of specific user set data. Under the hood we are using the Google Gemini API. Because we use LLMs to perform the analysis, we are able to provide our findings in automatically generated human understandable text.

Use Cases

  • Crop Yield Prediction: Analyze historical and current satellite data to forecast crop yields, helping farmers make informed decisions about planting and harvesting.
  • Water Management: Use Earth observation data to assess soil moisture levels and optimize irrigation schedules, promoting water conservation in agriculture.
  • Pest and Disease Detection: Leverage multispectral imagery to identify early signs of crop stress, pests, or diseases, enabling timely interventions.
  • Climate Change Adaptation: Analyze long-term climate trends to help farmers adapt their practices to changing environmental conditions.
