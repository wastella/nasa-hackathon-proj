AgroEye Use Cases

Irrigation Guidance

Farmers can use recent rainfall data to adjust their irrigation schedules. If it has rained significantly in recent days, they may decide to delay irrigation until later in the season when soil moisture levels are lower. This helps conserve water and prevent over-irrigation, which can be harmful to crops.

Humidity and Disease Risk

Recent high humidity could indicate an increased risk of fungal diseases or other moisture-related issues. Our app can help farmers warn farmers to inspect crops for early signs of disease.

Harvest and Storage Decisions

If the past 24 hours were especially humid or hot, the app could suggest delaying the harvest to avoid moisture damage or taking precautions to dry or store crops properly..

Wind Speed and Direction for Spraying

Farmers can use recent wind data to determine if conditions were suitable for spraying pesticides or fertilizers, helping to avoid waste or drift that could harm other areas.

Frost Damage Risk

If recently, the data shows unusually low temperatures, the app can alert farmers to potential frost damage risks, allowing them to take protective actions for crops.
