About AgroEye

Our Mission

AgroEye is dedicated to connecting farmers with satellite insights for sustainable agriculture. We empower farmers with NASA's geospatial datasets and analytics to make informed decisions about water usage, predict crop yields, and mitigate environmental risks.

AgroEye was made to compete in the challenge Leveraging Earth Observation Data for Informed Agricultural Decision-Making in the 2024 NASA Space Apps Challenge.

The Project

AgroEye was developed as part of the NASA Space Apps 2024 Challenge. Our project combines cutting-edge technology with NASA's extensive datasets to provide valuable insights for the agricultural sector.

Key Features:

  • LLM Analysis: Utilizing Google Gemini API for advanced data analysis and human-readable insights.
  • Data Visualization: Interactive charts and graphs for easy understanding of complex agricultural data.
  • NASA Datasets: Leveraging geospatial data from NASA to provide accurate and timely information.
